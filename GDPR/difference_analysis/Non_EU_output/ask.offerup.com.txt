Info other people see about you
Your public profile will be visible to other buyers and sellers on OfferUp. It displays selected information about you, including the following:
Your name and profile photos
Your approximate location
All your active posts
Your followers and the people you’re following
Your username and profile photo may be imported automatically when you create your account if you log in with Facebook.
